Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

At duvv.me, we take your privacy seriously. Like, actually seriously. We collect only what's necessary to make the platform work.

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information to make the app work:

  • Account Information: Your chosen username and recovery code (encrypted)
  • Content: Questions (duvvs) you create and responses you receive
  • Media: Audio recordings and drawings you upload (stored securely)
  • Usage Data: Response counts, views, and timestamps
  • Technical Data: IP address for security and rate limiting

2. How We Use Your Info

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain your account
  • Display your public profile and questions
  • Store and deliver anonymous responses
  • Prevent spam, abuse, and security threats
  • Improve our service and user experience

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using:

  • Database: Appwrite Cloud (encrypted at rest)
  • File Storage: Appwrite Storage for media files
  • Authentication: JWT tokens with secure cookies
  • HTTPS: All data transfers are encrypted

4. Data Sharing

We DO NOT sell your data. Period. We only share data when:

  • Required by law (with valid legal process)
  • Necessary to prevent harm or illegal activity
  • You explicitly share your duvv link publicly

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all data we have about you
  • Delete: Deactivate or delete your duvvs anytime
  • Export: Request a copy of your data
  • Control: Manage who can respond to you

6. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies only for:

  • Keeping you logged in (essential)
  • Remembering your preferences (theme, settings)
  • No third-party tracking or analytics cookies

7. Children's Privacy

Duvv.me is intended for users 13 years and older. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has created an account, please contact us.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We'll notify you of major changes by updating the date at the top and posting a notice on the platform.

9. Contact Us

Questions about privacy? Reach out:

  • Email: duvv.me@gmail.com
  • Contact Form: duvv.me/contact
